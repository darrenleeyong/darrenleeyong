Hackfest Hackathon (NUS).

Second Hackathon at Hackfest Hackathon. This hackathon was on a larger scale and had more focus on tech. Competition was really tough and judging criteria was fairly different. Our product was 'Percusso' - an analytic tool for music makers where users will critique and comment on different parts of the song.

Hackathons are fun and great for learning.

Despite the controversy of hackathons, I still think that hackathons are a great avenue to think out of the box, apply creativity in problem solving and a test of your limits. If you want to learn more about group dynamics, get ideas and inspiritation, and know your skill level, hackathons are just the place to be at.
