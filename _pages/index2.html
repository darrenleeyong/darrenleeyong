Darren is a self-taught Full Stack UX Designer and a Junior (3rd year) student of National University of Singapore, majoring in Communication & New Media specializing in Design. Combining data-driven methodologies, creativity, and a user-centered approach into problem-solving, he develops an understanding of the product without going through the pain of building it. Darren also loves the outdoors and has scaled Mt. Snowdon, the tallest mountain in Wales, UK, and trekked in several National Parks (Lassen, Yosemite, Muir Woods) in California.
