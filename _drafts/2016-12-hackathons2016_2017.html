NewAge Hackathon 2.0 (2016).

First ever hackathon. NewAge Hackathon 2.0 was a good and beginner-friendly hackathon with a core focus on social themes such as depression and loneliness for the elderly or youth. I teamed up with Gao Fei, our pitch guy, and Sathish, junior Android Dev, for this hackathon and thought of an idea inspired by Pokemon Go (which just came out very recently). The idea was targeted at the elderly to go to checkpoints in Singapore and do actvities, collecting points which can be redeemed for discount vouchers or the such. We got an honorary mention by the chairman of SG100, Kok Chin Tay, to work on the idea.
